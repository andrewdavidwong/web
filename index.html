Welcome

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Philosophy at the University of California, San Diego. Prior to this, I was an undergraduate in the Department of Philosophy at Washington University in St. Louis. My primary areas of philosophical focus are ancient philosophy and epistemology. I am currently working on a dissertation on the nature and scope of Pyrrhonian Skepticism.
