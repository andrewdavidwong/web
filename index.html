Welcome

I am a Ph.D. candidate in philosophy at the University of California, San Diego and Community Manager of the Qubes OS Project. Prior to this, I completed my bachelor’s degree in philosophy at Washington University in St. Louis. My primary areas of philosophical focus are epistemology and ancient philosophy. I am currently completing a dissertation on the nature and scope of Pyrrhonian Skepticism.
